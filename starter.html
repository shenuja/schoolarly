Starter Programme

Schoolarly's aim is to help build 'great schools' - schools that nurture learning beyond the classroom. We are looking to partner with trend-setting schools that are ready for the internet generation. The Schoolarly Starter Programme is a step in that direction.

Benefits

  • Avail unlimited technical support over Skype and email
  • Receive help in email setup and data migration
